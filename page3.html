Shaolin Soccer (2001)

so shaolin soccer is my number 2 and honestly some days it feels like number 1. the whole idea is just so stupid in the best way possible, like a kung fu monk decides to play soccer and uses his powers to kick the ball insanely hard and i just love it. the special effects are ridiculous but thats the point and it works so well. i remember watching this with my cousins and we were all just losing it the whole time. its one of those movies that puts everyone in a good mood no matter what.
