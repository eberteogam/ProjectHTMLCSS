CJ7 (2008)

ok this is my number 1 and its not even close. CJ7 is about this really poor dad and his kid and the kid finds this weird little alien thing and i dont even know how to explain it but this movie made me cry and laugh in the same 10 minutes. the dad character is so good, like he has nothing but he still tries so hard for his son and you can just feel it. i dont usually get emotional over movies but this one gets me every single time no matter how many times ive seen it. if you havent watched it just go watch it.
