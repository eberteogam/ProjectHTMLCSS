Kung Fu Hustle (2004)

kung fu hustle is my number 3 but honestly its still one of the best movies ive ever seen. its set in like 1940s China and theres this guy who just wants to be in a gang but he keeps failing at it and then it turns out theres all these hidden kung fu masters living in this random apartment. the action scenes are so over the top its unreal. i think what i like most is how it starts kind of slow and weird and then by the end your jaw is on the floor. every time i rewatch it i catch something new and thats rare for me.
