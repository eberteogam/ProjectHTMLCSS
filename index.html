Stephen Chow

Director • Actor • Comedian

ok so Stephen Chow is probably my favorite director ever, not even gonna lie. he was born June 22, 1962 in Hong Kong and the guy just gets comedy in a way nobody else does. his style is called mo lei tau which is basically just pure chaos and absurdity and its honestly so funny every time. i grew up watching his stuff with my family and i still go back to it. like theres something about his movies where youre laughing the whole time but then out of nowhere it hits you emotionally and youre not ready for it. that combo is hard to pull off and he just does it every time.

"I just want to make people laugh. That's my whole goal."

My Top 3 Movies

  • ① CJ7
  • ② Shaolin Soccer
  • ③ Kung Fu Hustle
Why Stephen Chow?

honestly the first time i saw Kung Fu Hustle i was like a kid and i had no idea what i was watching but i was glued to the screen the whole time. after that i just went and watched everything he made. there's no one else like him, every movie has this thing where it starts out goofy and then by the end you actually care about the characters. he's the reason i started actually paying attention to who directs movies.
